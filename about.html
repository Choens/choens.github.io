I am a social worker, program evaluator, and general-purpose geek, living in Albany, NY with my (lovely) wife, Karen, and daughter. I am a board member (and code-monkey) with the Upstate Artists Guild and a trail builder (and rider) with the Saratoga Mountain Biking Association .

Professionally, I tend to spend far too much time staring at a computer screen. I am an experienced program evaluator with experience in child welfare, elder-care, health care, housing, and juvenile justice. I earned my MSW at SUNY Albany's School of Social Welfare . I currently work for IPRO and work on the Health Homes evalution for the New York State Department of Health.
